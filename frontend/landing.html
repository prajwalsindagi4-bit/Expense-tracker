We keep your
information safe

We recently overhauled our entire security infrastructure to patch critical vulnerabilities and ensure enterprise-grade protection.

-
Zero-Trust JWT Auth
We enforce cryptographically signed, self-destructing JSON Web Tokens for mathematically secure sessions.
-
Military-Grade Hashing
Passwords are salted and aggressively hashed using bcrypt to prevent Rainbow Table decryption attacks.
-
Server-Side Lockdown
Full protection against Broken Access Control (IDOR), XSS, and brute-force with strict rate limiting.
